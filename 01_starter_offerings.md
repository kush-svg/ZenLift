# Zenlift — Starter Offerings Definition
**Internal Document | Version 1.0**
Last Updated: May 2026

---

## Why Only Three Offerings To Start

Zenlift is a three-person team. More offerings = more delivery complexity, more client education, and slower onboarding. Three offerings create a clean conversation: every prospect can be placed into one of these three starting points. They are designed to stack — a client who buys Offering 1 is a natural candidate for Offering 2, and both are candidates for Offering 3.

---

## Offering 1 — Digital Starter

### One-Line Description
A clean, credible digital presence that makes the business findable, trustworthy, and easy to contact.

### What Problem It Solves
Many local businesses either have no website, a dead Facebook page, or an outdated JustDial listing as their only digital identity. When a potential customer searches them, they either can't find them or are not impressed. This offering fixes that baseline problem.

### Scope
- 3 to 5 page website **OR** a single focused landing page (depending on business type)
- Mobile-first design (most local traffic is phone-based)
- Basic on-page SEO (title tags, meta descriptions, local keywords)
- Google Maps embed and directions link
- WhatsApp Click-to-Chat button (primary CTA for Indian local businesses)
- Contact / inquiry form with email notification
- Google Business Profile audit + basic optimization (name, category, photos, hours)
- Written copy for all pages (Zenlift writes it — client does not need to provide copy)
- 1 round of revisions

### Deliverables Checklist
- [ ] Live website or landing page hosted on client's domain (or subdomain if they don't have one)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized design
- [ ] WhatsApp CTA integrated
- [ ] Google Maps block
- [ ] Contact form with email/WhatsApp notification
- [ ] Basic meta SEO setup
- [ ] Google Business Profile reviewed and updated
- [ ] Handover document with login credentials and brief instructions

### Pricing
| Variant | Scope | Price (INR) |
|---|---|---|
| Landing Page Only | 1 page, focused CTA | ₹7,000 – ₹10,000 |
| 3-Page Website | Home, Services, Contact | ₹12,000 – ₹16,000 |
| 5-Page Website | Home, Services, About, Gallery/Reviews, Contact | ₹18,000 – ₹24,000 |

> **Positioning note:** Do not lead with price. Lead with outcome. "A page that gets you 5-10 new inquiries per month from people already searching for what you do."

### Delivery Timeline
- Landing Page: 5–7 working days
- 3-Page Website: 8–12 working days
- 5-Page Website: 12–18 working days

### What Is Included
- All design and copy work
- Template-based build (Framer, Webflow, or WordPress — chosen based on client needs)
- Domain connection (if client already has domain)
- 30 days of post-launch support for minor fixes (not redesigns)
- Google Business Profile basic optimization

### What Is NOT Included
- Logo design or brand identity work (if they need a logo, quote separately — ₹3,000–₹5,000)
- Custom coded website from scratch
- E-commerce / booking system integration
- Domain or hosting purchase costs (client bears this — typically ₹1,000–₹3,000/year)
- Ongoing content updates or maintenance after 30 days
- Social media content
- Paid ad campaigns

### Upsell Path
After delivery, offer Offering 2 (Lead Capture + Chatbot) and/or Offering 3 (Growth Support).

## Offering 2 — Lead Capture + Chatbot Setup

### One-Line Description
A system that captures inquiries automatically and responds instantly — even when the owner is unavailable.

### What Problem It Solves
Most local business owners are their own front desk. They miss calls, reply to WhatsApp messages hours later, and have no record of who inquired and what happened. This offering installs a basic inquiry capture and instant-response layer so no lead falls through the cracks.

### Scope
- Lead capture form (embedded on website or as standalone link)
- WhatsApp chatbot OR website chatbot (using tools like ManyChat, Tidio, or WATI depending on client's setup)
- FAQ automation — up to 15 common questions and answers
- Lead notification — instant WhatsApp or email alert to owner when a lead comes in
- Basic lead log — Google Sheet or Notion board where all leads are recorded automatically
- 1 month of post-setup support

### Deliverables Checklist
- [ ] Lead capture form live (embedded or standalone)
- [ ] Chatbot configured with welcome message, FAQ flow, and lead handoff
- [ ] 15 FAQ responses written and loaded
- [ ] Notification system tested and working (owner gets alerted per lead)
- [ ] Lead tracking sheet set up and auto-populated
- [ ] 30-minute walkthrough session with client on how to view and manage leads
- [ ] Handover document with login credentials and usage guide

### Pricing
| Variant | Scope | Price (INR) |
|---|---|---|
| Lead Form + Notification Only | Form + email/WhatsApp alert to owner | ₹6,000 – ₹8,000 |
| Chatbot + Lead Capture | Full chatbot + FAQ + lead form + tracker | ₹12,000 – ₹18,000 |
| WhatsApp Business API Setup + Chatbot | Includes WATI/ManyChat account setup | ₹18,000 – ₹25,000 |

> **Note on API costs:** The WhatsApp Business API (via WATI, ManyChat, etc.) has a monthly platform fee paid by the client — typically ₹1,500–₹4,000/month depending on volume. Make this clear before selling this variant.

### Delivery Timeline
- Lead Form + Notification: 3–5 working days
- Full Chatbot Setup: 7–12 working days
- WhatsApp API + Chatbot: 10–15 working days

### What Is Included
- Chatbot setup and configuration (not custom-coded — tool-based)
- Up to 15 FAQ flows written by Zenlift
- Lead form design and integration
- Notification pipeline (owner gets alerted)
- Google Sheet / Notion lead tracker
- 1 month of minor adjustments (adding FAQs, updating responses)
- Walkthrough session

### What Is NOT Included
- Custom-coded chatbot or AI chatbot (this is rule-based/flow-based)
- CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
- Monthly chatbot management after the first month (offer this as an add-on)
- Paid WhatsApp API platform fees (client pays directly to tool provider)
- Content for paid campaigns
- More than 15 FAQ flows at launch (additional flows billed at ₹300–₹500 per flow)

### Recurring Add-On Option
Offer a monthly maintenance retainer at ₹2,000–₹3,000/month for:
- Adding/editing FAQ flows as business needs change
- Monitoring chatbot performance
- Monthly lead report summary

### Upsell Path
Clients on this offering naturally need Offering 3 (Growth Support) to fill their lead funnel. Chatbot is useless without traffic. Pair them.

## Offering 3 — Growth Support (Monthly Retainer)

### One-Line Description
Ongoing monthly support for local visibility, lead generation, and digital presence management.

### What Problem It Solves
A website and chatbot are infrastructure. Growth Support is the engine. This offering ensures the business is consistently visible, running promotions, and attracting new inquiries every month — without the owner having to think about it.

### Scope
- Social media content: 8–12 posts/month (graphics + captions) for Instagram and/or Facebook
- Google Business Profile management: regular posts, offer updates, review response
- 1 local digital campaign per month (Meta Ads or Google Ads) — ad budget paid separately by client
- WhatsApp broadcast message: 2 per month (for promotions, reminders, or seasonal offers)
- Monthly performance report: what was posted, how the campaign performed, lead counts
- Monthly check-in call: 20–30 minutes

### Deliverables Each Month
- [ ] 8–12 social media posts (designed + captioned)
- [ ] Google Business Profile: 4 posts, review replies
- [ ] 1 campaign setup and managed (Meta or Google)
- [ ] 2 WhatsApp broadcast messages written and sent
- [ ] Monthly report shared via PDF or Google Doc
- [ ] Check-in call completed

### Pricing
| Tier | Scope | Price/Month (INR) |
|---|---|---|
| Light | 8 posts + GBP management + report | ₹8,000/month |
| Standard | 12 posts + GBP + 1 campaign + 2 WA broadcasts + report | ₹14,000/month |
| Full Growth | Standard + 2 campaigns + additional content formats | ₹20,000/month |

> **Ad budget is separate.** Client pays Meta or Google directly. Minimum recommended budget: ₹3,000–₹5,000/month for meaningful reach in local areas.

### Contract Terms
- Minimum 3-month commitment recommended (explain: campaigns take time to learn and optimize)
- Monthly billing on 1st of each month
- 30-day cancellation notice

### What Is Included
- Content creation (graphics using Canva Pro / Figma templates)
- Caption writing
- Post scheduling
- Campaign setup and management (1 ad campaign per month)
- Google Business Profile maintenance
- WhatsApp broadcast copy
- Monthly report
- 1 check-in call

### What Is NOT Included
- Paid ad budget (client pays Meta/Google directly)
- Video production or reels (offer separately at ₹2,000–₹5,000 per reel)
- Website changes (minor fixes billed separately, major redesigns quoted separately)
- Customer handling, responding to DMs or comments beyond standard GBP review replies
- Additional campaigns beyond the tier limit

### Important Internal Note
Growth Support is Zenlift's most time-intensive offering per month. Until the team has SOPs and content templates for each niche, do not take more than 4–5 Growth Support clients simultaneously. Quality drops fast if this is overloaded without systems in place.

## Offering Stack Logic

```
ENTRY POINT
    │
    ▼
Digital Starter (₹7k–₹24k one-time)
    → Gives them a digital presence
    │
    ▼
Lead Capture + Chatbot (₹6k–₹25k one-time)
    → Makes that presence capture and respond to leads
    │
    ▼
Growth Support (₹8k–₹20k/month)
    → Fills the funnel with ongoing traffic and visibility
```

A client that takes all three is paying ₹35,000–₹70,000 upfront + ₹8,000–₹20,000/month recurring. That is a strong LTV for a local business client.

## Pricing Philosophy

- Never price by hours. Price by outcome and perceived value.
- Always quote a range, not a single number, in early conversations. It lets you qualify budget without locking yourself in.
- Offer 2–3 package options. People rarely pick the cheapest when they see three tiers.
- If a client pushes back on price: do not discount immediately. First ask what outcome they are hoping for. Often the conversation reveals they misunderstood the value.
- For very small or first clients: it is acceptable to offer a small discount in exchange for a testimonial, case study permission, and a referral. Document this explicitly so it does not become standard practice.

## What Zenlift Does Not Sell (Yet)

To stay focused and deliverable with a 3-person team, Zenlift does not currently offer:
- Custom-coded applications or software
- Full-service SEO campaigns (on-page only as part of Offering 1)
- Bulk content creation (50+ posts/month)
- Enterprise CRM implementation
- Logo / brand identity packages as a standalone service
- Video production as a standalone service

These may be added later as team capacity grows or as documented demand justifies them.
